01

Logistics Business

We provide one-stop food import/export, delivery and wholesale services from Japan to Singapore. Our handled Japanese food products range from processed foods to rice and meat, delivering to stores while maintaining quality regardless of temperature. Currently, we have secured over 60,000 pallet storage spaces across ambient, chilled and frozen warehouses. By utilizing our own refrigerated trucks, we have established a cold chain that enables consistent low-temperature delivery from Japan to local destinations. In addition to regular container shipping, we can arrange transportation methods suited to your needs, including air freight.

02

Retail Business

At A&J HAKKO PTE LTD, we believe in not just transporting goods, but maximizing product appeal through local communication. That's why we support Japanese food sales in Singapore stores. Leveraging our broad sales channels including department stores, grocery stores and online platforms, we conduct store staff interviews, create sales floors, and arrange events like tasting sessions. This enables us to capture authentic local feedback and reflect it in sales strategies.

03

Event Support Business

Beyond logistics and retail, we support Japanese food exhibitions at local department stores. From export operations including customs clearance from Japan to the venue, to quality-maintained delivery and post-event support, we provide one-stop service. Furthermore, through close collaboration with our subsidiary A&J Malaysia, we are expanding our services beyond Singapore to across Asia.

04

Investment business

To provide opportunities to enjoy Japanese food locally, we invest in Asian food and beverage businesses in Singapore, Thailand, and China. We will continue leveraging our logistics expertise to support businesses from multiple angles, helping more people worldwide become familiar with Japanese food products.
