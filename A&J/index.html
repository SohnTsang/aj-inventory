About Us

Bringing Asia Closer Together

A&J HAKKO PTE LTD is dedicated to delivering the authentic taste and quality of Japanese food — not only within Japan but throughout Asia.
Rooted in the long-established Hakko Unyu Co., Ltd., a logistics company with over 70 years of history, we have developed the expertise to transport Japanese food products overseas while maintaining their freshness and quality.
By leveraging our logistics know-how and adapting to diverse local practices, we support the smooth and reliable distribution of Japanese food across Asia.
